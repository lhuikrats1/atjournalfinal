# Monte Carlo Simulation

_Exported from analytics/monte-carlo/page.tsx_

## Purpose

Allow users to simulate thousands of trade equity paths based on configurable win rate, risk‑reward ratio, and trade size. Helps assess probability of ruin and expected median equity.

## Features
- Adjustable parameters via sliders.
- Real‑time chart of equity curves across trials.
- Summary stats: median final balance, ruin probability, max drawdown.
