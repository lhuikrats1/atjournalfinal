# Reports

_Exported from reports/page.tsx_

## Purpose

Generate downloadable PDF and CSV reports summarising trade performance, risk metrics, and session analytics.

## Features
- Select a date range and report type.
- Export to PDF (styled to match the AtJournal brand) or CSV for external analysis.
- Includes charts and tables directly from the analytics engine.
